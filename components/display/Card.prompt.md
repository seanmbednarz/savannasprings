Surface container for grouping content — product tiles, testimonials, feature blocks, form panels.

```jsx
<Card variant="elevated" hoverable padding="lg">
  <h3>Impression Plus Series®</h3>
  <p>Soft water for you and your household.</p>
</Card>
<Card variant="water">…</Card>
<Card variant="brand">…</Card>
```

Variants: `elevated` (white + soft shadow, default), `soft` (navy tint), `outline`, `water` (spring tint), `brand` (navy, white text). `padding` none/sm/md/lg, `hoverable` lifts on hover, `as` to change the tag.
