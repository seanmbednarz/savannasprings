Primary action control — use for any clickable commitment (Get a quote, Schedule a test, Submit). Pill-shaped and brand-confident.

```jsx
<Button variant="accent" size="lg" iconRight={<ArrowIcon/>}>Get a free water test</Button>
<Button variant="primary">Request service</Button>
<Button variant="outline">Learn more</Button>
```

Variants: `primary` (navy, the default), `accent` (sun yellow — the brand's hero CTA), `water` (spring blue), `secondary` (soft navy tint), `outline`, `ghost`.
Sizes: `sm` `md` `lg`. Props: `shape` ('pill' | 'rounded'), `block`, `loading`, `disabled`, `iconLeft`, `iconRight`.
